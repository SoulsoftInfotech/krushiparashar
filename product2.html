  NATURAL SOLUBLE EXTRACT FOR PLANT STARTUP

An unique soil conditionera naturally occurring organic substance. Highly concentrated form of water soluble acid base humus with potassium lignin, Chelates plants nutrients and promotes the conversion of in soluble nutrient into available forms to plants. Acts as plant growth stimulators and soll structure enhancer. Provide a valuable source of organic carbon for soil micro organism. It enhances water reduction & air-water relationship in the soil. It prevents loss from leaching and acts as absorbent for nutrients. Increases the porosity of the soil (better action). Completes assimilation of chemical fertilizers and nutrients. Field test conducted indicated that yield will go up by 25% to 30%, if applied along with other fertilizers & result in simultaneous reduction in the chemical fertilizer consumption by 25% to 35% It increases crop yield in an eco friendly way since this product contains lot of carbon humus mass.
